Beginners are welcome:

For those who have zero programming experience or aren't a CS major: we encourage you participate! This is a great opportunity to challenge yourself. Pick a language or technology that you're passionate about and learn it! You'll be rewarded for it -- check out the "Best Beginner Project" award below.

Total freedom:

Create anything you want, on your schedule, and with a team of your choice (or go solo if that's your thing).

Great personal & professional experience:

Working on a personal project is a fun and effective way to gain experience developing software, learn some new skills, and improve your ability to work with a team. For those exact reasons, it also looks really good on a resume!

Win prizes:

At the end of the 6 weeks, submit a 2-minute video of your creation, get votes from CSC members, and win up to $1k in prizes!

Open to all majors and experience levels.

There are no restrictions on who can participate. Regardless of your experience level or major, CSC Hacks is a great opportunity to get some hands-on experience with programming!

No creative restrictions.

There aren't any categorical awards or competition topics -- just a simple first, second, and third place. Make something you're passionate about, and it will show!

Works with your schedule.

There are no time requirements and no due dates (except for the end of the competition), so work on your project when you want and for however long you want!
